Current Research Projects
Predictors of Infant Mortality

The goal of this project is to determine the significance of various predictors of infant mortality and whether this significance differs between whites and blacks, while also considering the impact of state and/or region of residence.
Narrated Experiences of Breastfeeding

The goal of this project is to determine how women resist negative discourses surrounding breastfeeding in public, specifically by using public forums and private support groups on Facebook.
Past Research Projects
Breastfeeding Initiation Trends, Shelby County
This project used Shelby County Health Department data to analyze breastfeeding initiation trends by race and age of the mother, with particular attention paid to disparities within various groups. This project was presented at one regional conference. Accepted for publication.
Breastfeeding Attitudes and Experiences in Memphis, TN
I interviewed three women in order to determine their experiences with infant feeding and the attitudes they held toward various aspects of breastfeeding. I also interviewed one locally practicing lactation consultant which helped to inform my recommendations for increasing breastfeeding success and exposure. This project was presented at one national conference for undergraduate students.
