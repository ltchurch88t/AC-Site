About Me

My name is Anna Claire Church. I am currently working on my Master's degree in Sociology at the University of Memphis with an expected graduation date of May 2019. Afterwards, I will be pursuing further education at a Ph.D. granting university, where I hope to be an active researcher of reproductive health and disparities. This includes studies of the social conditions and constraints of breastfeeding, the impacts of stress due to persistent poverty, racism and/or discrimination on maternal and infant health and reproductive outcomes, trans reproductive health, and others. Thank you for visiting my website! Please reach out with any questions or concerns that you may have.
